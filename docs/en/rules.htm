DDN Cave Rules and Regulations

Revision: 2026-06-05


1. Definitions

1.1. DDN Cave is a social, non-commercial initiative featuring shared-use Property for Digital Nomads, operating on a cost-sharing model. The Property includes:
- a shared space for work and Events;
- high-speed internet;
- private rooms with beds for sleeping;
- a kitchen for cooking, eating, and food storage;
- showers and restrooms;
- depending on the specific Property, it may include additional amenities, such as access to a free swimming pool, sauna, gym, meal preparation for Guests, laundry/drying facilities, shared transport, etc - please refer to the specific DDN Cave Property descriptions.

Property may be leased or otherwise provided by the property owner (a company or a private individual) or their Representative through a deal with Representative of the DDN Community. Digital Nomads residing in a DDN Cave support the DDN Cave initiative, for example, by participating in expenses for the Property proportionally to the number of days stayed, or by contributing to DDN in some other manner.

DDN Cave is not a business of any kind and does not provide commercial services, including hospitality/hotel services!

1.2. Digital Nomad is a professional who travels from country to country and conducts their activities via the internet, for example:
- works remotely for an organization;
- engages in scientific research;
- provides creative, technical, marketing, or other digital services both individually and as part of groups;
- conducts international entrepreneurial activities.

1.3. DDN is an independent international community of Distributed Digital Nomads. Some of the DDN members are available in the team list of one of the iol apps by “DDN vpnf” available on the P/PS section of the NiXeL app.

1.4. Property Owner's Representative is an individual who holds the right to provide Property on behalf of the owner for the DDN Cave through an agreement with a DDN Representative.

1.5. DDN Representative at the DDN Cave is an individual who has entered into an agreement with the Property Owner's Representative for a specific Property for DDN Cave. Different Properties may have different DDN Representatives, and they appoint a Caring-man for the specific Property from among the members of the DDN Community.

1.6. Guest is a Digital Nomad and a foreign national residing in the Property who has completed registration and obtained access to the Property in accordance with the DDN Cave Instructions. Guests are self-serving within the Property.

1.7. Event is a collaborative activity with a defined theme involving THREE or more people, which may take place with visitors on-site or online.

1.8. Instructions are provided in a separate document, as well as within the software systems utilized by DDN Cave.

1.9. Caring-man has special access and plays manager role in the Property, giving tips to Guests.


2. Strictly Prohibited in the Property

2.1. Smoking any substances, including electronic devices.

2.2. Consumption of alcohol and narcotics.

2.3. Bringing illegal substances, prohibited items, or illegally obtained items into the Property.

2.4. Gambling.

2.5. Entering the Property without registering as a Guest or an Event attendee. The only exceptions are a 10-minute Property show for potential Guests conducted by the Caring-man, or official maintenance personnel.

2.6. Remaining in the Property after the registered checkout time.

2.7. Bringing unauthorized external parties into the Property.

2.8. Transferring keys, Wi-Fi access credentials, or other devices to anyone.

2.9. Wearing footwear that has been used outside the Property.

2.10. Touching or using other people's belongings and food without the owner's permission, excluding shared-use items.

2.11. Removing other people's stuff from the Property, except for keys given you for the duration of your stay. This may be treated as theft!

2.12. Engaging in any form of violence.

2.13. Breaking, destroying objects, or causing damage to the Property. You are liable to compensate for any damages or restoration expenses.

2.14. Providing false information within DDN systems.

2.15. Gaining unauthorized access to electronic devices, such as the Wi-Fi access point, the internal computer network, security cameras, etc.

2.16. Violating any additional rules specific to the given Property.

2.17. Minor children (underage individuals).

2.18. Animals and vehicles.

2.19. Making noise, talking loudly, or listening to devices without headphones.


3. Cleanliness and Hygiene

3.1. General cleaning of the Property and garbage removal is handled by a hired Cleaner every working day.

3.2. Leave outdoor footwear outside the entrance door.

3.3. Do not wear dirty clothes or outdoor footwear inside the Property.

3.4. Dispose of garbage in the rubbish bin of the Property or the building's waste container.

3.5. Do not leave trash, bottles, boxes, packaging, etc., on the floor, bed, table, wardrobe, or other surfaces.

3.6. Wipe up liquids immediately if something is spilled.

3.7. Do not leave the toilet, sink, or shower floor dirty after use.

3.8. Do not scatter clothing around the Property.

3.9. Perform personal hygiene procedures such as clipping nails and cutting hair, skincare, etc., exclusively in the shower rooms.

3.10. Use only designated rooms/areas for washing and drying laundry.

3.11. Label personal hygiene products and items in accordance with Instruction #2.

3.12. Do not put feet or step on tables.


4. Bedroom

4.1. Use only the bed and bed linen assigned to you personally.

4.2. Do not make noise or use devices without headphones.

4.3. Do not disturb sleeping or working Guests.

4.4. Coordinate air conditioning settings with the other Guests in the bedroom.

4.5. Do not turn on the lights during daylight hours or after 24:00.

4.6. Do not block walkways with personal belongings.

4.7. Change your bed linen for clean sheets every 7-10 days.

4.8. Do not eat or store food items in the bedroom.

4.9. Do not use toilets or shower rooms that are not designated for the Guests of your specific bedroom.

4.10. Keep doors closed at all times.

4.11. Vacate the bedroom with all your belongings before 12:00 PM on the day of checkout.


5. Kitchen and Dining

5.1. Wash dishes, cooking areas, and dining spaces using detergents immediately after use. The sink must be left completely empty and clean afterward.

5.2. Do not leave or store food in shared cookware or dishes.

5.3. Return dishes to their designated storage space in the kitchen after use, or place them on the drying rack after washing.

5.4. Do not take dishes out of the kitchen into other rooms.

5.5. Use the kitchen and shared tableware strictly for food preparation and dining.

5.6. Label personal kitchen utensils and food items in accordance with Instruction #2.

5.7. Do not store food items in the refrigerator for more than THREE days.

5.8. Use the freezer exclusively for food products that strictly require freezing.

5.9. Do not use the fridge for items and products that do not require cold storage.

5.10. Foods have higher priority in the fridge than all liquids, such as water, sodas, juices, milk, etc. In case of a lack of space for food, any liquid in bottles or cartons (including other Guest’s) may be removed from the fridge and placed next to the trash bin, if no one claims them immediately.

5.11. Do not leave expired or spoiled food items in the kitchen.

5.12. Unlabeled items, items not intended for refrigeration, and food left in the refrigerator for more than THREE days will be discarded by the Cleaner.

5.13. The Cleaner cleans out the fridge every week.

5.14. Store foods and/or meals only in designated areas: shelves, compartments, fridge, etc. Remove and set aside any food and meals (including other Guest’s) left in unauthorized places (tables, floor, chairs, gas stove, cooking surfaces, etc.) for more than 1 hour.

5.15. Do not leave the gas/electric stove turned on and unattended.


6. Safety and Security

6.1. Find out and save the emergency numbers for local rescue services and medical assistance prior to checking into the DDN Cave Property.

6.2. Do not violate the security requirements of the territory or the building management.

6.3. Do not grant other people access to your personal devices or valuables.

6.4. Do not give the keys to the Property or storage lockers to other people.

6.5. Do not answer or open the entrance door to the Property in response to knocks or doorbells.

6.6. Avoid conflicts and fights.

6.7. Follow the instructions for registration and use of storage lockers.

6.8. Observe safety regulations when dealing with electrical appliances, open flames, tools, bunk/high beds, and other potentially hazardous objects.

6.9. Keep the contact details of your next of kin written on paper in English (and preferably in the local language as well) with you at all times.

6.10. Turn off all electrical devices when leaving a room or the entire Property for more than 30 minutes if no one remains inside.

6.11. The Caring-man, DDN Representatives, the Property Owner and their representatives are not liable or responsible in any way for your health, life, or stuff.


7. Shared Workspace

7.1. Maintain silence in the shared workspace at all times.

7.2. Use headphones whenever operating personal devices.

7.3. Speak as softly as possible if a conversation is necessary.

7.4. Events may only be held within the Shared Workspace and must not exceed a duration of TWO hours.

7.5. Coordinate and schedule Events in accordance with Instruction #3.

7.6. Event visitors are not permitted to enter any other rooms, except for the Shared Workspace and its nearest restroom.

7.7. The execution of an Event must not violate the rules and policies of the building hosting the Property.


8. Recommendations in the Property

8.1. Guests arrive from different countries, and English is used for international communication, just as it is within DDN.

8.2. Maintain valid health and life insurance for the entire duration of your stay at the DDN Cave Property.

8.3. Do not allow political views, gender, racial, linguistic, religious, or other differences to cause discomfort to anyone staying at the DDN Cave Properties.

8.4. Study local traditions, culture, and the customs of the population where the Property is located.

8.5. Whenever possible, assist other Guests and share treats - this supports a friendly atmosphere within the DDN Cave.


This document and the Instructions may be updated, and Guests will be notified of them.
